Any enemy within the field takes damage, Deals Geo DMG based on Albedo's DEF (Transient Blossom)

  • Can be aimed if held

    Normal Attack

    Performs 5 rapid attacks

    Elemental Skill

    Creates 1 Chisel Light-Mirror, Grants Dendro Infusion and Projection Attacks

    Chisel Light-Mirror:

    Grants Dendro Infusion

  • If there are no Mirrors on case, Creates 2 Mirrors
  • Can aim and teleport by Holding skill

    • Mirror Duration: 4s

    Projection Attack:

    Deals Dendro DMG alongside of Normal Attack. DMG depends on the number of Mirrors present

  • 1 Mirror: Deals 1x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM
  • 2 Mirrors: Deals 2x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM
  • 3 Mirrors: Deals 3x Dendro DMG based on ATK and EM

    • Projection Attack Interval: 1.6s

    CD: 18s

    Elemental Burst

    Consumes current Mirrors and Create Dendro Damaging AoE Field depending on the number of Mirrors consumed

  • Instances are 4/6/8/10 if 0/1/2/3 Mirrors consumed

    • 2 seconds after Burst, Creates 3/2/1/0 if 0/1/2/3 Mirror were consumed

    Energy Cost: 70

    CD: 18s

    Ascension Passive 1

    Charged and Plunging Attack creates 1 Mirror

    CD: 12

    Ascension Passive 4

    Increases Projection Attack and Burst DMG by 10% for every 100EM

    Max DMG: 100%/1000EM
